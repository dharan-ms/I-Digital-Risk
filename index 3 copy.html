About IDR

Where Industry Expertise
Meets Academic Rigour

The Institute of Digital Risk is an industry-led training and deployment organisation built to address the growing gap in qualified digital risk, cybersecurity, and technology governance professionals across regulated sectors.

IDR partners with leading UK universities and academic institutions to deliver structured, practitioner-led programmes — combining theoretical frameworks with hands-on project experience in real organisational environments.

Our graduates and deployed professionals are equipped to navigate complex risk landscapes, implement established frameworks, and lead transformational digital security initiatives from day one.

Service Pillars

Three Pillars of Digital
Risk Excellence

Academy

Training & Bootcamps

Structured training programmes and intensive bootcamps designed for students, career changers, and professionals entering digital risk. Our curriculum combines industry frameworks with live project work, mentored by senior practitioners.

Innovation & Incubation

AI Governance &
Risk Innovation

We develop new digital risk models, AI governance frameworks, and emerging cyber resilience strategies. IDR incubates ideas from academic research and industry insight into scalable, deployable solutions for modern organisations.

Advisory Services

Frameworks &
Risk Advisory

Supporting organisations to design and implement established risk and security frameworks including NIST CSF, ISO 27001, and NIS2. Our advisors bridge the gap between regulatory compliance and operational resilience.
